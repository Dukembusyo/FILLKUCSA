Why join KUCSA?

KUCSA is open to all university and college students of Kitui County and beyond. Membership is the cornerstone of our success, offering opportunities for leadership, networking, and personal growth.

join us

Ordinary Members

Enrolled students from Kitui County with full voting rights and leadership eligibility.

Diaspora Members:

Students from outside Kitui County who are welcome to participate in all activities, although without voting rights.

Alumni Members:

Graduates who continue to contribute to KUCSA through voting and leadership roles.

Membership

Join the KUCSA Family

Membership Rights & Responsibilities:

Rights: Access to all association activities, voting in general meetings (as applicable), and eligibility for leadership roles.

Responsibilities: Payment of a membership fee (as determined by the executive committee), active participation in meetings and programs, and adherence to the KUCSA Code of Conduct.

Membership Code of Conduct:

Members

Members are expected to uphold the values of unity, vision, and progress. Respect for fellow members, active involvement in programs, and ethical behavior are mandatory. Failure to comply may result in disciplinary action, including suspension or expulsion.
